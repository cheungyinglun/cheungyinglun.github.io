Course Outline
======
* Primer of statistics and probabilities
  * Introduction to probability theory
  * Random variables, expectations and conditioning
* Counting process
  * Poisson process
  * Renewal process
* Markov chains
  * Discrete-time Markov chains
* Brownian motion
  * Random walks
  * Brownian motions
